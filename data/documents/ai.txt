Neural networks are computing systems inspired by the brain.
They consist of layers of neurons that transform inputs into outputs.
Deep learning is a subset of machine learning using deep neural networks.
Backpropagation is used to train neural networks.
Activation functions introduce non-linearity.
Gradient descent optimizes model parameters.
AI models require large datasets.
Data preprocessing improves model performance.
GPUs accelerate neural network training.
Transformers are widely used in NLP.
